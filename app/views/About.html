About




The WikiWeb is a network of crowdsourced connections between content. We like to think of it as a map of the Internet, but most people prefer to think of the WikiWeb as a place to find suggestions for what to read next on the Internet.


Accessing the WikiWeb requires downloading the Chrome plugin. Once that's done, you're good to go: you'll see the WikiWeb navbar at the bottom of your browser with interesting links to explore. Occasionally, you might even want to add your own.


The more people who contribute to the WikiWeb the better it gets. If you have any quesitons or suggestions please send them to hello@wikiweb.org. Let's make the Internet better, together!
