About




Surfing the web is broken.

Our solution is to create a Map of the Internet, which we call the WikiWeb. The WikiWeb is a crowdsourced collection of links from any one site to another. Any time you on an page and you're thinking "I know a site that is similar to this one - maybe other people would be interested in it too?", you should help your fellow web surfers by adding that connection to the WikiWeb.


The WikiWeb is an open graph of information and all content is public-by-default. It's a big web out there and we're just getting started. We look forward to building it with you, and hearing your feedback and ideas along the way.
